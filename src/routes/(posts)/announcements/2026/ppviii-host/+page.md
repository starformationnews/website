To all concerned,

Following e-mail discussions among the members of Scientific Advisory Committee (SAC) of Protostars and Planets VII (PP7), we have decided to set an open call for the organizers of _**Protostars and Planets VIII**_ (PP8). If you are willing to organize PP8, please visit the following website and submit your proposal. The proposal should be in pdf format and no more than two pages long. The names of the main organizers of the proposal will be posted [on the PP7 webpage](https://ppvii.org/) shortly after submission. If multiple proposals are submitted, the PP7 SAC will review them and select one. The deadline of the submission is 30th of June 2026. Thank you for your consideration.

Please submit your applications [here](https://forms.gle/xsKdTin1WnrR6m2d8).
 
Best wishes,

Shu-ichiro Inutsuka, on behalf of the PP7 SAC
